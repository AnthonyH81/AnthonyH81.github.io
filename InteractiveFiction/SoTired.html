Intervention

“I’ve been doing this for a while now. Usually, things were alright. As alright as it could be at least. I did my best and helped out as many people as I could. I know you can’t save everyone but I had to try. I’ve given everything I had to be a hero to all. So to fail so miserably for this long hurts because all these years wasted only for me to be the reason why people died.” You said as the words poured out of you. Sometimes, talking to someone is all that’s needed for tension to be released. “I want to get back into it,” you sighed. “But I’m so tired and I don’t know who I am if I’m not helping people, you know?”
